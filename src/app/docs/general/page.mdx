# Introduction

Find documentation for our APIs, detailed concept rundowns, or get help with specific engine plugins.

## What does Rivet provide?

- Everything is accessible from an easy to use [GUI, CLI, or API](/docs/general/gui-cli-api)
- [Dynamic Servers](/docs/dynamic-servers/index) for auto-scaling game lobbies
- [Flexible matchmaker](/docs/matchmaker/index) for placing players in lobbies with no wait times
- [CDN](/docs/cdn) for hosting assets and webpages with a custom domain or provided rivet.game subdomain
- [Game Guard](/docs/dynamic-servers/concepts/game-guard) for DDoS mitigation and managed WebSocket SSL & TCP+TLS termination
- Streamlined DevOps for teams
- Unified logging & monitoring & analytics
- No downtime deploys with easy rollbacks
